Booking and Reservations

At L'Île O Spa, we've designed a seamless booking and reservation process to ensure your experience with us is as convenient as it is relaxing. Here's a step-by-step guide on how you can book your spa experience through our website:

  • Choose Your Reservation Date Begin by selecting your preferred date for your spa reservation. We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate your busy lifestyle.
  • Select Your Preferred Location: L'Île O Spa is proud to have two exquisite spa locations for you to choose from. Pick the one that suits your preferences and convenience.
  • Choose Your Desired Room:Our spa locations feature a variety of beautifully appointed rooms, each offering a unique ambiance. Select the room that resonates with your relaxation goals.
  • Pick Your Preferred Time Slot:To ensure a tranquil atmosphere, we offer carefully spaced time slots for our guests. Choose the time that works best for you.
  • Select Your Desired Formula:Customize your spa experience by choosing from our range of spa formulas. Whether you're looking for a soothing massage, a rejuvenating facial, or a comprehensive spa package, we have something to meet your needs.
  • Explore Our Activities: Enhance your spa day by exploring our additional activities. We offer a wide array of options, including meals, drinks, therapies, and various recreational activities. Tailor your experience to create the perfect day of relaxation.

Payment Options: You have two convenient payment options when booking your reservation with us:

Pay Online: If you're booking for two guests, simply select "Number of Guests: 2" during your reservation, and you'll be prompted to pay for both guests online in a single transaction.

Pay Onsite: Alternatively, you can choose to pay for one guest's reservation online, and the second guest's reservation can be settled when you arrive at our spa location. Our friendly staff will be happy to assist you with the payment process.
